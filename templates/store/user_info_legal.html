User Information Legal Enquiry Guide

At Khalab, we believe in transparency. This guide explains how we collect and protect user information, and how we handle legal enquiries related to customer data.

User Information

We only collect essential details — such as name, phone, email, and address — to process orders and provide support. Your data is always stored securely and never shared without authorization.

Legal Enquiries

In case of lawful requests from authorities, Khalab will provide access to relevant data as required by law. Whenever legally possible, customers will be informed about such disclosures.

Customer Rights

You have the right to enquire how your information is used. For any concerns, please contact us at {{ contact_email }}.

Khalab is committed to safeguarding your privacy while complying with all applicable legal requirements.
