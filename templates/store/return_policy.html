Return & Exchange Policy

At Khalab, your satisfaction is our top priority. Please review the rules below to ensure a smooth shopping experience.

General Rules

  • Customers must check their product in front of the delivery man at the time of delivery.
  • No return requests or issues will be accepted after the delivery man has left.
  • If a product is found damaged during checking, customers may return or exchange it immediately.

Outside Dhaka

  • For deliveries outside Dhaka, returns are accepted only if the product is damaged.
  • Customers must notify the courier immediately upon receiving a damaged product.
Note: Always check your parcel in the presence of the delivery person to avoid future disputes.
